Refound Policy

1. Refund amounts vary depending on the timing of the cancellation:
 • Cancellation 30 days or more before the departure date: Full refund of the reservation fee
 • Cancellation 29 to 20 days before departure: 70% refund of the total travel cost
 • Cancellation 19 to 10 days before departure: 50% refund of the total travel cost
 • Cancellation 9 to 1 day before departure: 30% refund of the total travel cost
 • Cancellation on the day of departure or no-show without notice: No refund

2. In case of cancellation due to the travel agency's fault, a full refund and additional compensation will be provided as separately announced.

3. Refund requests are processed based on the cancellation request date, and the refund processing period may take up to 14 days.

4. Exceptions to the refund policy may apply in cases of force majeure such as natural disasters, illness, or accidents, upon submission of valid documents.

For further inquiries, please contact our customer service center.

Privacy Policy

Travel Explorer values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applicable in Ireland and the EU.

Personal Data Collection and Purpose
 • We collect only the minimum personal information necessary such as name, contact details, email address, and passport number for booking and providing travel services.
 • Purpose of collection: processing bookings, customer support, legal compliance, service improvement, and customer communications.

Storage and Retention
Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes it was collected for and in accordance with legal obligations.
After the retention period, data is securely deleted.

Third-Party Sharing
Personal information is shared only with third parties essential for travel service delivery (airlines, hotels, insurance providers) and only with your consent or as required by law.
We do not share personal data for marketing or unrelated purposes.

Your Rights
You have rights under GDPR including accessing, correcting, deleting your data, restricting processing, withdrawing consent, and lodging complaints with the Data Protection Commission.
Requests can be made via email or customer service and will be handled promptly.

Children’s Privacy
For customers under 16, parental or guardian consent is required for data processing.

Cookies and Tracking
Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience. You may control cookie settings through your browser.

Travel Explorer strives to protect your information and will notify you of any changes to this Privacy Policy in advance
For further inquiries, please send us a message by contact us form.

Insurance

Coverage
 • This insurance policy covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost or delayed luggage, and travel-related accidents during the insured trip.  • Coverage is valid only within the dates and destinations specified in the policy.

Eligibility
 • The insured must be a resident of Ireland, and the policyholder must provide accurate personal information at the time of application.
 • Children under 16 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

Exclusions
 • Pre-existing medical conditions unless declared and accepted in the policy.
 • Injuries or losses resulting from extreme sports, illegal activities, or substance abuse.
 • Losses due to war, terrorism, or natural disasters unless specifically covered.

Claims
 • Claims must be submitted within 30 days of the incident with complete documentation including medical reports, receipts, and police reports if applicable.
 • Fraudulent claims will result in policy termination and possible legal action.

Cancellation and Refunds
 • Insurance premiums are generally non-refundable once coverage starts, except in cases of policy denial or duplicate purchase.

Data Protection
 • Personal data collected for insurance purposes will be processed in compliance with Ireland’s GDPR regulations and used solely for underwriting, claims processing, and customer service.

Contact
 • For questions or to file a claim, customers should contact Travel Explorer’s insurance liaison or the insurance provider directly.
